Why it is important to clean a conditioner

Save 50% electricity on heating or freezing

Clean conditioner is 50% quickly keep the temperature you wish. It does not waste additional time, electricity (and money!) for heating or freezing.

Kill Bacteria

Dirty conditioner consists of thousands of bacteria that can lead to allergies, running nose and increase risk of diseases. Clean conditionerguarantees only air.

Long-Term Durability

Cleaning twice a year you give your conditioner additional 10 years of work

Use Less Freeze

One conditioner without cleaning A shower uses 10 to 25 gallons of water, while taking a bath can use up to 70 gallons. You could save a lot on water bills!

*Important detail:

According to instructions of air conditioner producers cleaning must be done twice a year. Customers may do it at any season, no matter if it is cold or hot in the street.
